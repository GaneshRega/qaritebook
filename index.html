For Students

Founded in 2019 in Hyderabad, Pragmatiq operates on three pillars: Tech Partnerships, Product Platforms, and Academy. We focus on Healthcare (Clinally®), Agriculture (Landlordz), and Education (PurpleGene®).

The COE helps source funds for research, supplements academic processes with expert resources, and improves accreditation scores (NAAC/NBA). We facilitate collaboration with startups for real-world projects.

We foster an environment of Upskilling, Research, and Innovation through FDPs and workshops. Our platform allows faculty to monitor student performance via detailed analytics and conduct coding sessions.

Our expertise covers a modern portfolio including Java, Python, Node.js, Angular, React, and AWS/Azure deployment, with strong capabilities in Generative AI.
